Retaining Wall Solutions

Safe, flexible earth-retention systems for constrained ROW and challenging ground. We support projects across India, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Africa and Europe with ISO-aligned QA/QC and export logistics.

Why choose Oyexperts

  • Up to 30–40% cost saving vs RCC walls
  • Seismic-friendly, drainable facings reduce hydrostatic pressure
  • Modular facings: precast panels, blocks, gabions, vegetated geocells
  • Design assistance, material supply and site supervision

System Types

MSE/RE walls with geostrips or uniaxial geogrids, gravity gabion walls, gravity geocell walls, and reinforced soil slopes with green facings.

Typical Applications

Interchanges, abutments, ramp walls, approach embankments, landfill bunds and hill road widening.
